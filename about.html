Our Mission and History

Development History

The original site of the ARGO Museum of Modern Art is a greenhouse in the People’s Park. During the construction of the museum, the external glass walls of the stadium were preserved, and large-scale reconstructions were performed inside the stadium. The stadium not only retains its superior natural lighting conditions and the park's excellent landscape, but also meets the museum's professional requirements after adding more facilities.
The total area of the museum is 4,000 square meters; the exhibition area is the first floor and second floor exhibition halls, with a total area of nearly 1800 square meters. All exhibition areas are equipped with professional temperature and humidity control facilities.

Our Mission

ARGO wants to introduce high-quality international contemporary art and design into America and use it to promote global cultural exchange.
We collect and study America modern art and design in order to become an information center of America modern art. Also, ARGO wants to develop public awareness of modern art and design.

Your support helps research and conservation at the ARGO, so we can present our exhibitions and events.
